# Book: "Vibe Coding: Build Anything with AI"

> The definitive guide to AI-assisted software development for non-technical people — and the story of a movement that made building software feel like music.

---

## Concept

**Title:** Vibe Coding: Build Anything with AI  
**Subtitle (working):** The Non-Programmer's Guide to Building Real Software  
**Author:** Kyle Rosebrook  
**Format:** Trade paperback + ebook  
**Target Length:** 60,000–80,000 words  
**Target Publisher:** O'Reilly Media (primary), No Starch Press (secondary), self-published (fallback)  
**Target Audience:** Non-technical people curious about building with AI — same audience as the workshop

---

## Why This Book Needs to Exist

Most programming books are written for people who want to become developers. This one is for people who just want to build something.

There are hundreds of books on Python, JavaScript, React. There are zero books that say: "Here's how to go from an idea in your head to a real app running at a real URL — without learning to code first." That book needs to exist. This is it.

The workshop proves the concept. Every cohort has people who've never written a line of code and who ship four apps in one day. The book scales that experience to anyone with a laptop and an idea.
