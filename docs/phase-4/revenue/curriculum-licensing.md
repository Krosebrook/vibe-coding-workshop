# Curriculum Licensing — For Coding Bootcamps & Training Organizations

> License the Vibe Coding Workshop curriculum to deliver the same beginner-first, AI-powered learning experience at your school or organization.

---

## What Is Curriculum Licensing?

Curriculum licensing lets a bootcamp, training company, or educational organization teach the Vibe Coding Workshop under their own brand — using our curriculum, templates, and tools, with Kyle's support and quality standards.

It's not a franchise. You run your school. We give you the curriculum and the backing.

## Licensing Terms

### What You Can Do
- Teach the curriculum under your organization's name
- Charge participants your own pricing
- Customize examples and context to fit your audience
- Translate materials (with approval)
- Adapt the timing and format to your delivery model

### What You Cannot Do
- Use the "Vibe Coding Workshop" trademark as your primary brand
- Sublicense the curriculum to another organization
- Remove attribution to Kyle Rosebrook / Vibe Coding Workshop
- Share curriculum materials publicly (outside of your paid participants)
- Claim ownership of the curriculum

### Quality Standards
All licensees agree to:
- Maintain the "zero experience required" positioning
- Use the approved tool stack (Cursor, Claude, v0, Vercel, Supabase)
- Collect participant feedback using the provided survey template
- Share anonymized participant NPS with Kyle annually

## Licensing Process

1. **Inquiry** — Email us with organization name, type, and intended use
2. **Discovery call** — 30-minute call to assess fit and discuss logistics
3. **Proposal** — We send a licensing proposal and agreement draft
4. **Pilot workshop** — Optional 1-day pilot before committing to a full year
5. **Agreement** — Sign the Curriculum License Agreement
6. **Onboarding** — Instructor training + onboarding call
7. **Launch** — Run your first cohort with our support

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use this for a bootcamp that lasts several weeks?**  
A: The curriculum is designed for one day, but it can be expanded. Growth and Enterprise licenses include custom module additions.

**Q: What if my participants aren't beginners?**  
A: The curriculum is designed for beginners, but it works for experienced professionals too. More advanced participants move faster and explore further.

**Q: Do you offer instructor certification separately from licensing?**  
A: The Regional Instructor Program (see [regional-workshops.md](../community/regional-workshops.md)) covers individuals who want to teach under the Vibe Coding Workshop brand. Curriculum licensing is for organizations.

**Q: Is there a trial period?**  
A: We encourage a pilot workshop before signing a full-year license. Pilot workshops can be arranged with a one-time $500 fee.
