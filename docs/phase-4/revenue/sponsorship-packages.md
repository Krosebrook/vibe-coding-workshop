# Sponsorship Packages — AI/Dev Tool Companies

> Sponsorship tiers and packages for companies who want to reach the vibe coding community.

---

## Why Sponsor Vibe Coding Workshop?

The Vibe Coding Workshop reaches a unique audience: **non-technical people who are actively building with AI tools for the first time.** These are the early adopters, the curious operators, the founders who just realized they can build their idea — and they're forming brand preferences right now.

Sponsors get access to:
- Workshop participants at the exact moment they're choosing their tools
- Community of alumni who continue building after the workshop
- A team that uses and believes in AI development tools

---

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Background** | Non-technical (marketers, designers, founders, operators) |

## Sponsorship Tiers

### 🥉 Bronze — Community Supporter
**$500 / cohort**

For companies that want visibility in the vibe coding community without deep integration.

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Logo in workshop materials | Slide deck, participant handout footer |
| Website credit | Listed on vibecodingworkshop.com/sponsors |
| Social mention | 1 post per cohort (LinkedIn + Twitter/X) |
| Participant count | Up to 20 participants per cohort |

---

### 🥈 Silver — Workshop Partner
**$1,500 / cohort**

For companies that want hands-on visibility during workshop day.

| Benefit | Details |

## Sponsorship Policies

### Who Can Sponsor
- AI coding tools (editors, models, assistants)
- Deployment and hosting platforms
- Developer tooling companies
- Education and upskilling platforms

### Who Cannot Sponsor
- Competitors to the workshop itself (other coding bootcamps)
- Companies with known data privacy violations
- Financial products, insurance, or unrelated industries

### Sponsor Content Review
All sponsor-provided content (slides, materials, scripts) must be reviewed and approved at least 1 week before the workshop. We reserve the right to decline content that is misleading, overly promotional, or inconsistent with the vibe coding ethos.
