# Community — Overview

> Phase 2 community initiatives for Vibe Coding Workshop alumni.

---

## Why Community Matters

The workshop is one day. The community is ongoing.

Participants who stay connected after the workshop build more, improve faster, and refer others. A strong alumni community is also the most credible marketing for future cohorts — real people building real things.

Phase 2 community infrastructure includes a Discord server and an alumni newsletter.

## Guiding Principles

**Useful, not noisy.** Participants get enough emails. Every message should deliver value or celebrate someone's work.

**Peer-to-peer, not lecture.** The goal is alumni helping each other, not just top-down content from Kyle. Design for that.

**Build in public.** Encourage alumni to share what they're building — in the Discord, in the newsletter, on their own social channels. The best marketing is real work.

**Low barrier, high value.** The Discord should be easy to join and immediately useful. The newsletter should be worth reading every month.

## Community Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target (After 3 Cohorts) |
|--------|-------------------------|
| Discord members | 80%+ of alumni |
| Discord monthly active | 40%+ of members |
| Newsletter subscribers | 90%+ of alumni |
| Newsletter open rate | 45%+ |
| Alumni projects shared publicly | 10+ per cohort |
