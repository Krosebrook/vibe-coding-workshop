# Post-Workshop Follow-Up Email Sequence

> 3-email sequence sent to participants after the workshop. Goal: celebrate the win, keep them building, and build community.

---

## Sequence Overview

| Email | Subject | Send Timing | Goal |
|-------|---------|------------|------|
| Email 1 | You shipped it. 🚀 | Same night (within 3 hours) | Celebrate + resources |
| Email 2 | How's the building going? | 1 week after | Check in + next steps |
| Email 3 | One month in — what did you build? | 1 month after | Community + showcase |

---

---

## Email 1 — You Shipped It. 🚀

**Send timing:** Same night as the workshop (send by 9:00 PM)

**Subject line:** You shipped it. 🚀

**Preview text:** 4 apps. 1 day. All live. Here are your links + everything you need to keep building.


## Email 2 — How's the Building Going?

**Send timing:** 1 week after the workshop

**Subject line:** How's the building going?

**Preview text:** One week in. Here's a nudge to keep the momentum going.

---

### Plain Text Version

```
Hey [First Name],

It's been a week. How's it going?

This is the point where most people either keep building or slowly stop. I'd
love for you to be in the first group.

A few things that might help:

---

1. PICK ONE THING TO IMPROVE

Look at one of your 4 projects from workshop day. What's one thing that
bugs you about it? Now fix it. Use the prompting playbook.

Seriously — one thing. 30 minutes. That's it.

---

2. START SOMETHING NEW (IF YOU'RE READY)

What was the app idea you had that wasn't one of the 4 workshop projects?
Now you have the skills to start it.

Here's a starter prompt:

"I want to build [your app idea]. It should be a web app using HTML and vanilla
JavaScript. Before writing any code, outline the approach: what pages/screens,
what data I need to store, and what APIs I might need."

That conversation with Claude will take you from "I have an idea" to "I have a plan"
in about 15 minutes.

---

3. CONNECT WITH THE COMMUNITY

The Discord has been active. People are sharing what they're building.
[Discord link]

If you haven't joined yet — this is the nudge.

---

UPCOMING

[If there's a follow-up virtual session, mention it here]

Next workshop cohort: [date or "TBD — join the waitlist at vibecodingworkshop.com"]

---

Keep building.

Kyle
```

## Email 3 — One Month In — What Did You Build?

**Send timing:** 1 month after the workshop

**Subject line:** One month in — what did you build?

**Preview text:** A check-in, some inspiration, and an invitation.

---

### Plain Text Version

```
Hey [First Name],

It's been a month since workshop day.

I'm curious: what have you built since then?

If the answer is "nothing yet" — that's okay. Life happens. But if you still
have that idea sitting in the back of your head, this is your sign.

---

FROM THE COMMUNITY

Here's what some of your fellow workshop alumni have been building:

[Participant project spotlight 1 — name + description + link (with consent)]
[Participant project spotlight 2 — name + description + link (with consent)]
[Participant project spotlight 3 — name + description + link (with consent)]

If you've built something and want to be featured in the next email,
reply and tell me about it.

---

A RESOURCE YOU MIGHT NEED RIGHT NOW

The most common question I get a month out: "I want to add [feature] to my app
but I don't know where to start."

The answer is always the same: describe it to Claude.

"I have a web app that does [what]. I want to add [feature]. Here's the current
code: [paste]. Before writing code, outline the approach."

The ARCHITECTURE prompt pattern. Works every time.

---

NEXT STEPS

The next workshop cohort opens on [date]. If you have a friend, colleague,
or family member who would love this — share the waitlist:
[vibecodingworkshop.com]

If you want to go deeper, reply to this email and tell me what you're trying to
build. I'll point you in the right direction.

---

Keep shipping.

Kyle

---
Vibe Coding Workshop
Unsubscribe: [link]
```
