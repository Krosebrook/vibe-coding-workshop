# Pre-Workshop Onboarding Email Sequence

> 3-email sequence sent to participants after registration. Goal: arrive set up, excited, and with an idea in mind.

---

## Sequence Overview

| Email | Subject | Send Timing | Goal |
|-------|---------|------------|------|
| Email 1 | Welcome! Get ready for Workshop Day | 2 weeks before | Accounts + first install |
| Email 2 | Final Setup Check | 1 week before | All tools installed + tested |
| Email 3 | See you tomorrow! 🚀 | Day before | Last logistics + energy |

---

---

## Email 1 — Welcome! Get ready for Workshop Day

**Send timing:** 2 weeks before the workshop

**Subject line:** Welcome! Get ready for Workshop Day

**Preview text:** You're in. Here's what to do before you arrive.

---

### Plain Text Version

```
Hey [First Name],

You're registered for the Vibe Coding Workshop on [Date] at College of Lake County.
I'm Kyle — I'll be your instructor for the day.

Quick version: you're going to build and deploy 4 real apps in one day. Zero coding
experience required. All you need is a laptop and an idea.

Before you arrive, please create accounts on these 5 tools. They're all free.
It takes about 30 minutes. Do it now so workshop day is just building.

1. GitHub — github.com
   This stores your code and connects to your deployment.
   Create an account. That's it for now.

2. Cursor — cursor.com
   Your AI-powered code editor. Download and install it.
   When prompted to sign in, create a free account.

3. Claude — claude.ai
   The AI that writes your code. Create a free account.
   Free tier is fine for the workshop.

4. Vercel — vercel.com
   Click "Sign up with GitHub." That's it.

5. v0 — v0.dev
   Click "Sign up." Use your GitHub account.

That's the setup. If you hit any issues, reply to this email. I'll help.

One more thing: start thinking about what you want to build. The workshop projects
are structured (landing page, mood tracker, recipe finder, AI chat app), but the
best learning happens when you bring your own ideas to each one.

What's one thing you've always wished existed as an app?

See you on [Date],

Kyle Rosebrook
Vibe Coding Workshop
hello@vibecodingworkshop.com
```

## Email 2 — Final Setup Check

**Send timing:** 1 week before the workshop

**Subject line:** Final setup check — 7 days to go

**Preview text:** Make sure these 3 things are ready before workshop day.

---

### Plain Text Version

```
Hey [First Name],

One week until Vibe Coding Workshop. Quick check-in.

If you haven't created your accounts yet (GitHub, Cursor, Claude, Vercel, v0), do it
this week. The workshop moves fast — you don't want to spend the morning on signups.

Three things to verify before [Date]:

1. Open Cursor. Can you start a new project? (File → New Window → New Project)
   If Cursor won't open, email me now. Easy fix.

2. Open Claude at claude.ai. Can you type a message and get a response?
   If you're hitting a paywall, the free tier should work. Reply if not.

3. Open Vercel at vercel.com. Are you logged in with your GitHub account?

That's the checklist. 10 minutes tops.

---

WHAT TO BRING

- Your laptop + charger (non-negotiable)
- A notebook if you like writing things down
- An idea. Think: what would I build if I knew how to code?
- Yourself. No experience required.

---

LOGISTICS

Date: [Date]
Time: Doors open at 8:00 AM, we start at 8:30 AM
Address: College of Lake County, Grayslake, IL — [Room Number]
Parking: [Parking lot details]
WiFi: Provided — credentials will be on the whiteboard when you arrive

Lunch is provided. Bring your own coffee if you're particular about it.

---

Questions? Reply to this email. I read every one.

See you next week,

Kyle
```

## Email 3 — See You Tomorrow! 🚀

**Send timing:** Day before the workshop (send by 6:00 PM)

**Subject line:** See you tomorrow! 🚀

**Preview text:** Doors open at 8 AM. Here's everything you need.

---

### Plain Text Version

```
Hey [First Name],

Tomorrow is the day.

Doors open at 8:00 AM. We start at 8:30 AM sharp.

College of Lake County · [Building Name] · Room [Number]
[Full address]
Parking: [Lot / instructions]

---

QUICK CHECKLIST FOR TONIGHT

- [ ] Charge your laptop fully
- [ ] Download Cursor if you haven't (cursor.com)
- [ ] Log in to Claude at claude.ai
- [ ] Log in to Vercel at vercel.com
- [ ] Get a good night's sleep — it's a full day

---

WHAT TO EXPECT TOMORROW

You'll build 4 apps. Each one runs about 90 minutes. By 5:30 PM, all 4 will be live
at real URLs. At the end of the day, you'll have a portfolio you built in a day.

No experience required. If you've used ChatGPT before, you already have the core skill.
We're just going to go a lot further with it.

---

One last thing: bring an idea. It doesn't have to be polished. Just something you've
always wanted to build — even if it seems too big or too weird or too whatever.

The best apps that come out of this workshop are the ones that solve real problems
people actually care about.

See you tomorrow at 8 AM.

Kyle

P.S. If anything comes up tonight (tech issue, can't find the building, anything),
text or email me: hello@vibecodingworkshop.com
```
