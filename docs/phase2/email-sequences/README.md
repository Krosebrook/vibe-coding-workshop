# Email Sequences — Index

> All email templates used in Vibe Coding Workshop communications.

---

## Overview

These email sequences handle participant communications from registration through post-workshop follow-up. They're designed to prepare participants, reduce day-of friction, and keep the community engaged after the workshop ends.

---

## Sequences

| Sequence | File | Emails | When |
|---------|------|--------|------|
| Pre-Workshop Onboarding | [pre-workshop-onboarding.md](./pre-workshop-onboarding.md) | 3 | 2 weeks → day before |
| Post-Workshop Follow-Up | [post-workshop-followup.md](./post-workshop-followup.md) | 3 | Same night → 1 month after |

---

## Pre-Workshop Sequence (3 emails)

| Email | Subject | Send Timing |
|-------|---------|------------|
| Email 1 | Welcome! Get ready for Workshop Day | 2 weeks before |
| Email 2 | Final Setup Check | 1 week before |
| Email 3 | See you tomorrow! 🚀 | Day before |

**Goal:** Participants arrive with accounts set up, tools installed, and an idea in mind.

---

## Post-Workshop Sequence (3 emails)

| Email | Subject | Send Timing |
|-------|---------|------------|
| Email 1 | You shipped it. 🚀 | Same night |
| Email 2 | How's the building going? | 1 week after |
| Email 3 | One month in — what did you build? | 1 month after |

**Goal:** Participants stay connected, keep building, and feel part of the community.

---

## Email Platform Recommendations

| Platform | Best For | Cost |
|---------|---------|------|
| **Mailchimp** | Automated sequences, analytics | Free up to 500 contacts |
| **ConvertKit** | Creator-focused, clean UX | Free up to 1,000 subscribers |
| **Resend** | Developer-friendly, API-first | Free up to 3,000/month |
| **Gmail + BCC** | Simplest option for 20 participants | Free |

For a 20-person cohort, Gmail or Mailchimp free tier is sufficient.
