# Top 100 Learner Persona Portfolios

> Detailed profiles of the 100 most likely workshop participants. Used for curriculum design, marketing copy, and empathy mapping.

---

## How to Use These Personas

- **Curriculum design:** Ensure workshop content serves the range of backgrounds
- **Marketing copy:** Write to real people, not abstractions
- **Instructor prep:** Anticipate questions and blockers
- **Template selection:** Choose starter templates that resonate
- **FAQ development:** Identify common fears and objections
