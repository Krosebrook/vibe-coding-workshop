# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 2026  
**Effective Date:** March 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Vibe Coding Workshop ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or participate in our workshop.

We built this workshop for people learning to code, and we apply the same "keep it simple, don't be creepy" principle to our privacy practices.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Website Visitors

Our website (`index.html`) is a **static HTML page** with:
- No analytics scripts
- No tracking pixels
- No cookies
- No JavaScript that collects data
- No server-side logging beyond what GitHub Pages may collect

**We do not track individual visitors to the website.**

### 2.2 Waitlist Sign-Ups

The waitlist form on the website links to `mailto:hello@vibecodingworkshop.com`. When you click "Join Waitlist," your email client opens with our address pre-filled.

If you send us an email:
- We collect your **email address**
- We may collect your **name** if you include it
- We collect your **message content**

We do not add you to a mailing list without your explicit consent.

### 2.3 Workshop Participants

When you register for and attend the workshop, we may collect:
- Name and email address (for registration)
- GitHub username (for sharing workshop materials)
- Payment information (processed by third-party payment processor — we do not store card numbers)
- Work/background information you voluntarily share during registration

### 2.4 GitHub Repository

If you interact with our GitHub repository (open issues, submit PRs, leave comments), GitHub's own Privacy Policy governs that data. See: [https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Data | How We Use It |
|------|--------------|
| Email address | Respond to inquiries; send workshop updates you requested |
| Name | Address you in correspondence |
| GitHub username | Share workshop materials; give access to private repos |
| Payment info | Process workshop registration (via payment processor) |

We **never**:
- Sell your personal data
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for any purpose other than operating the workshop

## 4. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|----------------|
| Waitlist emails | Until the cohort fills or you request removal |
| Participant registration | 2 years after workshop completion |

To request deletion of your data, email [hello@vibecodingworkshop.com](mailto:hello@vibecodingworkshop.com) with the subject line "Data Deletion Request."

## 6. Security

We protect your data using:
- Email correspondence over TLS
- No storage of payment card numbers
- GitHub repository access controls
- No third-party data brokers

The website itself stores no personal data (it's a static HTML file).

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:
- **Access** — Request a copy of data we hold about you
- **Correction** — Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion** — Request deletion of your data
- **Portability** — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection** — Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact [hello@vibecodingworkshop.com](mailto:hello@vibecodingworkshop.com).

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our workshop is designed for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.
